# Video 002 Gold Label Guide

Video file:

```text
video-002-invoice-rework.mp4
```

First-pass label should be based on the video only.

Look for:

- what invoice or source document is opened
- what destination form/system is used
- any incorrect entry, correction, or repeated step
- whether the workflow is completed
- visible evidence for each field or action
- uncertainty around values that are hard to read

Do not hide rework. Rework is part of the observed workflow.
