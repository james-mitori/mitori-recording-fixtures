# Video 003 Gold Label Guide

Video file:

```text
video-003-support-interruption.mp4
```

First-pass label should be based on the video only.

Look for:

- the main support workflow
- any unrelated interruption
- whether the worker returns to the original task
- exact start/end of the interruption
- whether the main workflow is completed
- evidence supporting each boundary

Do not merge unrelated activity into the main workflow. Mark it as an interruption.
